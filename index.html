About Star Academy

Teaching that connects the syllabus to the real world.

Star Academy was built around one idea: science makes more sense when you can see where it's actually used. Miss Jyothi teaches GCSE Physics, Chemistry and Biology, plus A-Level Chemistry and Biology, drawing on her day-to-day experience as an NHS Anaesthetic Nurse.

The goal isn't just to get through the syllabus — it's to build genuine interest in a subject that's wonderful and deeply applied. Lessons often include hands-on kitchen science experiments, exploring real concepts like mixtures and heat loss with everyday materials, so ideas stick because they're seen, not just read.

Miss Jyothi particularly enjoys working with challenging students — the harder the connection, the more it sharpens her teaching, and that effort is what makes the breakthroughs worth it.

Lessons are built around explain → practise → assess — a topic is taught clearly, reinforced with homework, then checked with exam-style questions, so students don't just remember content, they can apply it under exam conditions.

Alongside school science, Miss Jyothi also mentors students and early-career professionals moving into Data Science, having built her own portfolio of machine learning projects from the ground up.
